Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian comfort food made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. It's the perfect dish for feeding a crowd and tastes even better the next day.

Ingredients:

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 250g ricotta cheese
  • 300g mozzarella, shredded
  • 50g parmesan, grated
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper and Italian seasoning to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 190°C
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan, add onion and garlic and cook until soft
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes and tomato paste, season and simmer for 20 minutes
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish
  7. Layer noodles, ricotta, meat sauce and mozzarella, repeat until all ingredients are used
  8. Top with remaining mozzarella and parmesan
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake for another 15 minutes
  10. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving
